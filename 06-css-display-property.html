Exercises

  1. Change one of the `block-element` divs to `display: inline;` and observe the effect. What happens to its width?
  2. Change one of the `inline-element` spans to `display: inline-block;` and give it a `width` and `height`.
  3. Toggle the `display` property of the `hidden-element` between `none` and `block` using your browser's developer tools.
  4. For the `.flex-item` elements, add `text-align: center;` and see how the text inside aligns.
